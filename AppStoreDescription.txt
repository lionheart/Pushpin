Features

* Search bookmarks blazingly fast, across titles, descriptions, URLs, or tags.
* Filter your own bookmarks by unread, starred, private, untagged, public. You can even filter with multiple tags!
* Share links with friends over Twitter, Facebook, or Messages.
* Send your links to Pocket, Instapaper, or Readability.
* View tag suggestions and autocompletions when adding or updating bookmarks.
* Browse Network, Popular, Wikipedia, Fandom, and Japanese feeds.
* View feeds for specific users, tags, or a combination of the two.
* Browse tags and view the number of times they've each been used.
* Add bookmarks by switching to Pushpin with a URL on your clipboard.
* In-app browser with support for Google, Readability, and Instapaper mobilizers.
